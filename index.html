Final Project

BONE FRACTURE DETECTION USING IMAGE PROCESSING

To detect the fracture in the bone is important in medical field. In this work X-ray/CT images are used to for bone fracture detection. Proposed system will detect the fracture of the bone from input X-ray image. The proposed system has three main steps namely, preprocessing, feature extraction and classification. Image Pre-processing involves image enhancement, edge detection. CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) for feature extraction and classification. The images are further classified into Normal or fractured bone. If bone is fractured then it locates the fractured area with the contour technique for line detection in the image. This model can achieve accuracy of 90%
